How It Works

The system organizes yoga practices across nine practice segments: Preparatory Practice, Breathing Practice, Suryanamaskara, Yogasana, Pranayama, Meditation, Additional Practices, Kriya (Cleansing Techniques), and Yogic Counselling.

When multiple diseases are combined, the system intelligently merges practices from each disease while removing duplicates and applying contraindications. This ensures safe and comprehensive treatment protocols for patients with multiple conditions.

The RCT Database tracks randomized controlled trials and studies, automatically calculating practice reliability scores based on the number of supporting research papers for each disease-practice combination.

All data is maintained with scientific rigor, ensuring evidence-based treatment protocols for both academic research and clinical application.
