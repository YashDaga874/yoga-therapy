How It Works

The system organizes yoga practices across five koshas (sheaths): Annamaya Kosa (physical), Pranamaya Kosa (energy), Manomaya Kosa (mental), Vijnanamaya Kosa (wisdom), and Anandamaya Kosa (bliss).

When multiple diseases are combined, the system intelligently merges practices from each disease while removing duplicates and applying contraindications. This ensures safe and comprehensive treatment protocols for patients with multiple conditions.

All practices are linked to research citations, maintaining scientific rigor and traceability for academic and clinical use.
